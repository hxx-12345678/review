# BEYONDVYU — GTM Strategy for Vadodara Restaurant/Cafe Market

## 1. Lead Segmentation by Digital Maturity

### Segment A: "Review Giants" (3000+ Google reviews, active social media)
**Leads:** Grand Thakar, Secret Kitchen, Honest, Daily Dose Cafe, Mocha, Barbeque Nation

**Digital Profile:**
- 3,000–15,000 Google reviews
- Active Instagram with 5K–50K+ followers
- Established brand recognition
- Already have some digital presence but no structured reputation management

**Pain Points:**
- Cannot manually respond to thousands of reviews
- Negative reviews buried in volume but still damaging
- No competitive intelligence on what rivals are doing
- No analytics on review trends over time
- Losing customers to competitors who respond to reviews

**GTM Angle:** "You already have the volume — now get the intelligence. BEYONDVYU helps you manage, analyze, and respond to reviews at scale. Your competitors are watching your reviews. Are you watching theirs?"

### Step 1: Research (15 min per lead)
- Check Google reviews count and rating
- Check Instagram activity and follower count
- Check Zomato/Swiggy presence
- Identify top 3 competitors
- Note any recent news or changes

### Step 2: First Contact (Email)
- Use the appropriate template above
- Personalize with specific data points
- Include a specific insight (e.g., "Your competitor responded to 50 reviews last month")

### Step 3: Follow-up (Phone call, 48 hours later)
- Reference the email
- Ask if they saw it
- Offer to send a personalized demo video
- Book a 15-minute call

### Step 4: Demo Call
