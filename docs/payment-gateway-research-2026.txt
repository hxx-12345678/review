1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

After exhaustive research of 23+ payment gateways in India (2026), the clear
recommendation for ReviewOS is:

  PRIMARY:  Razorpay (stay on current integration)
  ADD-ON:   VyaparGateway (for UPI-only routing to save 2%)
  FUTURE:   Negotiate Razorpay custom pricing at ₹5L+/mo GMV
  GLOBAL:   Add Stripe India alongside Razorpay at global launch

Key Findings:
  - UPI Autopay is the optimal recurring billing rail for sub-₹1000 txns
  - Razorpay's 2% flat + zero AMC + 93%+ success rate = lowest TCO at
    <₹5L/mo GMV despite higher headline rate than Cashfree
  - Cashfree's ₹4,999/yr AMC makes it MORE expensive below ₹2.08L/mo GMV
  - VyaparGateway's ₹300/mo flat (0% platform fee) saves ~₹11,800/mo vs
    Razorpay on ₹5L/mo UPI volume
  - No gateway beats Razorpay on subscriptions + developer experience
    for an early-stage SaaS in India

2.1 Razorpay (Already Integrated)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  PRICING

                     Max ₹15K (₹1L for insurance/SIP/MF)
                     PDN handled automatically
    Card eMandate:   ✅ Supported. Max limit: card network-defined
    eNACH:           ✅ Supported. Max ₹1Cr per debit
    Smart Retry:     ✅ Built-in dunning logic
    Subscription Fee: No separate charge (bundled in platform fee)

  SETTLEMENT
    Standard:        T+2
    Paid Upgrade:    T+1
    Instant:         Available at extra cost (₹5 + 1%)

  PERFORMANCE
    Success Rate:    93%+ (highest in India for UPI)
    Smart Routing:   ✅ Intelligent retry logic
    Uptime:          99.9%+

  PAYMENT METHODS
    Cards:           Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Diners, RuPay
    UPI:             All PSPs (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM)
    NetBanking:      72+ banks
    Wallets:         MobiKwik, Freecharge, Jio, Airtel, PayZapp
    EMI:             Card EMI + Cardless EMI
    BNPL:            LazyPay, Simpl, ICICI Pay Later
    International:   3% + GST (cards)
    Pay Later:       Supported

  PROS
    + Best developer SDK/API in India
    + Mature subscription engine with dunning + retry
    + 100+ payment modes in one integration
    + Zero setup fee, zero AMC
    + Highest UPI success rate (93%+)
    + RazorpayX ecosystem (payouts, capital, payroll)
    + Excellent documentation and webhook infrastructure
    + Fast onboarding (2-3 days)
    + No per-transaction fixed fee (critical for sub-₹1000)

  CONS
    - 2% flat is higher than Cashfree's 1.75% on UPI
    - T+2 settlement by default (T+1 costs extra)
    - No multi-currency on standard plan
    - International cards at 3% + GST

  USE CASE FOR REVIEWOS
    ✅ Ideal primary gateway. Sub-₹1000 subscriptions via UPI Autopay:
       Effective cost = 2% + 18% GST = 2.36% = ₹23.60 on ₹1000

4.13 Juspay (Payment Orchestration Layer)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  PRICING:     Custom per merchant. Typically 0.25%/txn OR ₹15K/mo flat

  WHAT IT IS:
    NOT a payment gateway. A payment orchestration platform that connects
    300+ payment providers through a single API. Adds dynamic routing,
    failover, and optimization on top.

  SUBSCRIPTIONS:
    ✅ Excellent. UPI Autopay, eNACH, card mandates, NACH.
    Best-in-class mandate infrastructure:
    - UPI Autopay: 99-year max tenure, auto PDN
    - eNACH: 3-4 day activation, ₹1Cr limit
    - Card mandates: token-based recurring
    - Retry module with 3 retry attempts

  PROS:
    + Single API to 300+ gateways
    + Dynamic routing (failover + cost optimization)
    + Best UPI Autopay infrastructure in India
    + Advanced retry engine
    + Tokenization vault
    + Supports Razorpay, Cashfree, PayU, PhonePe, BillDesk, etc.

  CONS:
    - EXPENSIVE for startups (₹15K/mo or more)
    - Adds latency layer (one more hop)
    - Overkill until significant volume
    - Requires technical expertise to configure routing rules
    - Not a direct replacement for a PG

  USE CASE FOR REVIEWOS
    ❌ Skip until >₹50L/mo GMV. Until then, Razorpay alone is
       simpler, faster, and cheaper.

7. TRUE COST ANALYSIS
================================================================================

The headline TDR is MISLEADING. True cost = TDR + AMC + failed transaction
revenue loss.

BREAK-EVEN ANALYSIS: Razorpay (2%, ₹0 AMC) vs Cashfree (1.75%, ₹4,999/yr AMC)

  Monthly GMV    | Razorpay (Annual) | Cashfree (Annual) | Winner
  ---------------|-------------------|-------------------|---------
  ₹50,000        | ₹12,000          | ₹15,449           | Razorpay
  ₹1,00,000      | ₹24,000          | ₹26,599           | Razorpay
  ₹2,08,000      | ₹49,920          | ₹49,920           | Tie*
  ₹5,00,000      | ₹1,20,000        | ₹1,12,999         | Cashfree
  ₹10,00,000     | ₹2,40,000        | ₹2,20,999         | Cashfree

  *Break-even point: ₹2.08L/mo GMV

  BUT: Success rate gap (93% vs 90%) changes the math significantly:

  At ₹2L/mo attempted GMV:
    Razorpay (93% success): ₹1,86,000 realized
    Cashfree (90% success): ₹1,80,000 realized
    GAP: ₹6,000/mo = ₹72,000/yr

  Even at ₹5L/mo where Cashfree "wins" on fees:
    Fee difference: ₹7,001/yr in Cashfree's favor
    Revenue lost to failures: ~₹18,000/mo = ₹2,16,000/yr
    NET: Cashfree costs ₹2,08,999/yr MORE in lost revenue

TOTAL COST OF OWNERSHIP FORMULA:
  TCO = (GMV × SuccessRate) - (GMV × TDR) - (AMC/12)
